# Kami - Bookmarks Directory Theme

> Warm canvas, ivory paper, ink-blue hierarchy, and document-grade restraint.

**Theme:** light

## Source

- Kami website: https://kami.tw93.fun/index-zh.html
- Kami repository: https://github.com/tw93/kami

This theme follows the Kami site design language at `https://kami.tw93.fun/index-zh.html`: warm paper surfaces, a single ink-blue accent, serif-led hierarchy, and almost no app-like elevation. The bookmark manager should feel like a quiet research dossier rather than a SaaS dashboard.

## Typography

- Display: Charter, Georgia, Songti SC, Source Han Serif SC, serif.
- UI: IBM Plex Sans, Microsoft YaHei, sans-serif.
- Mono: JetBrains Mono for compact counters only.
- Use serif for page titles, section headings, card titles, and primary statistics.

## Rules

- Prefer rings and hairline borders over raised app-card shadows.
- Keep blue ink as the only strong accent.
- Use Warm Sand for quiet control fills, not as a dominant page color.
- Keep repeated navigation controls compact, but let headings breathe like printed matter.
- Avoid glossy gradients, saturated secondary colors, and large shadows.
